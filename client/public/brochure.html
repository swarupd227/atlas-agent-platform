Why ATLAS

Built for enterprise AI operations.

Four principles that differentiate ATLAS from generic agent frameworks.

1
Compliance is structural, not optional
Policies are bound to agents, not to teams. Every execution generates an immutable audit trail — ready for regulators on day one.
2
Outcomes drive everything
Agents are not isolated scripts — they are built against outcome contracts. KPIs, SLAs, and risk tiers cascade from the contract down.
3
Humans stay in the loop — selectively
Configurable autonomy levels and intervention thresholds. High-stakes decisions escalate automatically; routine runs proceed without friction.
4
Industry context out of the box
Finance, insurance, logistics, healthcare — ATLAS ships with industry ontologies that pre-configure compliance rules, risk tiers, and evaluation criteria.
