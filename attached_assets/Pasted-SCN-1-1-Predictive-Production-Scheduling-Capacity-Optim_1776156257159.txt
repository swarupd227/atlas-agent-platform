SCN-1.1  Predictive Production Scheduling & Capacity Optimization
Advantive Products: Kiwiplan ERP/MES, Advantzware ERP   |   Industry: Corrugated, Folding Carton & Specialty Packaging
🔗 Atlas ↔ Advantive Co-Existence: Kiwiplan and Advantzware remain the systems of record for all orders, production data, and shop floor transactions. Atlas agents read live order queues, machine capacity, and shift data from Kiwiplan/Advantzware via MCP, apply multi-variable optimization reasoning, and propose optimized schedules — which are reviewed and committed back into Kiwiplan by the planning team. Atlas never bypasses the ERP; it amplifies it.

Business Problem
Packaging plant schedulers at corrugated and folding carton manufacturers manually balance 50–200 daily orders across die-cutters, corrugators, laminators, and finishing lines — juggling substrate availability, order priority, maintenance windows, and delivery commitments. Manual scheduling consumes 3–5 hours daily, produces sub-optimal machine utilization (typically 65–75%), and frequently misses on-time delivery windows when order mix shifts late in the shift.
Demo Narrative & Story Arc
Atlas deploys a real-time scheduling intelligence pipeline that reads live state from Kiwiplan/Advantzware, optimizes the shift schedule across machines and order priority, flags material constraints before they become production stops, and submits approved schedule proposals back into the ERP — turning the planner's role from manual juggler to exception handler.
1.	Order Intelligence Agent ingests the current order queue from Kiwiplan's ESP module — extracting order specifications, due dates, priority flags, and substrate requirements.
2.	Capacity & Constraint Mapper reads machine availability (planned downtime, maintenance locks), active shift rosters, and roll-stock inventory from Kiwiplan's inventory modules.
3.	Schedule Optimizer Agent applies multi-variable constraint optimization: balances OEE, changeover minimization, due-date adherence, and substrate efficiency across all available production lines.
4.	Schedule Proposal Agent generates a human-readable optimized shift schedule with change rationale, predicted OEE improvement, and risk flags — routing to the plant scheduler for approval before Kiwiplan commit.
