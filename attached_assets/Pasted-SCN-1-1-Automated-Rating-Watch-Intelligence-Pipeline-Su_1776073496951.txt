SCN-1.1  Automated Rating Watch Intelligence Pipeline
Sub-segment: Fitch Ratings   |   Companies: Fitch Ratings (Corporate & Structured Finance)
Business Problem
Fitch Ratings analysts manually monitor hundreds of corporate issuers daily — scanning SEC filings, news feeds, and CDS market data to identify candidates for rating actions. This process takes 3–5 analyst-days per issuer cohort and creates latency between signal emergence and formal rating review initiation.
Demo Narrative & Story Arc
Atlas deploys a four-agent pipeline that continuously monitors corporate issuers across the Fitch-rated universe. When market signals breach configurable thresholds, the pipeline automatically assembles a Preliminary Rating Action Memo for analyst review — compressing a multi-day manual process to under four hours.
1.	Market Signal Scanner polls Bloomberg/Refinitiv feeds and CDS spread data, flagging issuers showing material deterioration.
2.	Filing Intelligence Agent ingests the latest SEC 10-K/10-Q/8-K filings for flagged issuers, extracting key financial metrics and qualitative risk language.
3.	Peer Benchmarking Agent compares the flagged issuer's ratios against the Fitch peer cohort, computing deviation scores.
4.	Rating Action Memo Agent synthesizes all signals into a structured Preliminary Rating Watch memo with evidence citations, recommended watch direction, and trigger summary — delivered to the analyst review queue.
