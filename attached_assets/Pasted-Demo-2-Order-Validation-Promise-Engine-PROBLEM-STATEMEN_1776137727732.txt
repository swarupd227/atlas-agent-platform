Demo 2: Order Validation & Promise Engine
PROBLEM STATEMENT: Meridian Manufacturing accepts Quote Q-78432 and submits a $429K purchase order - marked RUSH for the Detroit plant delivery. However: (1) the order pushes Meridian's credit exposure to 92% of their $3.2M limit, (2) 12 of the turbine assemblies are only available across 2 warehouses (Chicago: 8, Atlanta: 4), and (3) the Detroit plant address fails validation (suite number mismatch). Today, this order sits in a queue for 2.3 days while 3 different teams manually resolve these issues.

DEMO OBJECTIVE: Show Atlas validating, resolving, and promising the order in under 3 minutes with full transparency into every decision.

AGENTS INVOKED: OTC-AGT-002 (Order Validation & Promise Agent), OTC-AGT-003 (Customer Credit & Risk Assessment Agent), OTC-AGT-004 (Inventory Availability & Promise Agent)

Key Interaction
The operations manager approves the credit increase with one click. The address panel shows a suggested correction ("Suite 1400" vs. submitted "Suite 140") and the manager confirms. The inventory panel is explored next.
