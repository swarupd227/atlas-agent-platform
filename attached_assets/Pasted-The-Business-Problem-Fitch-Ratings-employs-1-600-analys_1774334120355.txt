The Business Problem

Fitch Ratings employs 1,600+ analysts covering 33,300+ rated entities with $75.8T+ in rated debt. For US bank asset quality monitoring, analysts currently spend 40–60 hours per quarter per bank manually downloading FFIEC Call Reports, building Excel models for ratio analysis, reading earnings call transcripts for credit quality signals, and tracking news for emerging risks. There is no integration between structured financial data and unstructured text signals. By the time a deterioration pattern is identified, it has often already appeared in headlines.
The Asset Quality Early Warning System changes this by fusing structured regulatory data with NLP-derived signals from earnings transcripts, SEC filings, and news — producing a composite risk score per bank per quarter that detects deterioration 1–2 quarters before it surfaces in public metrics.

A2. Three-Layer AI Architecture


Layer 1: Structured Analysis (18 Financial Ratios)
Ingests quarterly FFIEC Call Reports (schedules RC-N, RC-C, RI-B, RC-R), SEC 10-K/10-Q filings, and FR Y-9C holding company data. Computes 18 ratios across three categories — Asset Quality, Earnings, and Capital — with threshold breach detection, trend acceleration analysis, and peer benchmarking against the G-SIB cohort.

#	Ratio	Formula	Source	Early Warning Threshold
R01	NPA Ratio	(Nonaccrual + 90+ PD) / Total Loans	RC-N	QoQ increase > 15bps
R02	NCO Rate	Net Charge-Offs / Avg Loans (ann.)	RI-B	Crosses 50bps or 2x peer
R03	Delinquency 30–89	30–89 Day Past Due / Total Loans	RC-N	QoQ increase > 20bps
R04	Delinquency 90+	90+ Day Past Due / Total Loans	RC-N	Any increase > 10bps
R05	Provision / Avg Loans	Provision for Credit Losses / Avg Loans	RI / RC-C	Exceeds 2yr avg by 25%
R06	ACL Coverage	ACL / NPAs	RC-N / RC-R	Drops below 100%
R07	ACL / Total Loans	ACL / Gross Loans	RC-R	Decline > 10bps QoQ
R08	CRE Concentration	CRE Loans / Risk-Based Capital	RC-C / RC-R	Exceeds 300% (regulatory)
R09	CRE / Total Loans	CRE Loans / Total Loans	RC-C	> 30% and increasing
R10	C&I NCO Rate	C&I NCOs / Avg C&I Loans	RI-B	QoQ increase > 10bps

Layer 2: NLP Signal Engine
Three distinct NLP models process unstructured text: (1) Earnings Transcript Sentiment — scores management tone on credit quality, forward guidance, and sector-specific concerns on a −2 to +2 scale per dimension; (2) Regulatory Filing Language Analysis — detects year-over-year changes in Risk Factors and MD&A sections, flagging new risk disclosures and strengthened cautionary language; (3) News Signal Classifier — classifies articles by entity, severity (routine/emerging/material/crisis), and relevance, tracking weekly volume for spike detection.

Layer 3: Composite Risk Score (0–100)
Weighted combination of all signal layers, producing a single score per bank per quarter:
Component	Weight	Description	Fitch Alignment
Structured Ratios	55%	18 ratios weighted by Fitch KRD relevance	Maps to Viability Rating factors
NLP — Transcripts	20%	Credit quality sentiment + forward guidance + sector signals	Supplements qualitative assessment
NLP — Filings	10%	Risk factor changes, MD&A language shifts	Validates regulatory signals
News / Macro	10%	News severity score + volume trend	Early detection layer
Peer Divergence	5%	Deviation from G-SIB peer median	Core to Fitch methodology

Score interpretation: 0–40 = Green (low risk), 41–60 = Amber (watch), 61–70 = Amber-High (active monitoring), 71–100 = Red (critical). Trajectory classified as improving (Δ < −3), stable (−3 to +3), or deteriorating (Δ > +3).

A3. SVB Backtesting — The "Wow Moment"

WHY THIS MATTERS
This backtesting case study is the single most important element for credibility with Fitch analysts. Without it, the demo is theoretical. With it, the demo is evidence-based. The system would have flagged SVB with a red alert FIVE MONTHS before the March 2023 bank run — before S&P, before Moody’s, before Bloomberg.
