Demo 4: AI-Powered Cash Application
PROBLEM STATEMENT: It's the last week of the month. NovaTech receives $42M in payments from 380+ customers via wire transfers, ACH, checks, and EDI 820 remittances. The current cash application team of 6 people manually matches payments to invoices - achieving only 62% auto-match rate, with the remaining 38% ($16M) requiring manual research averaging 4.2 hours per unmatched payment. A complex $2.3M payment from GlobalTech Corp covers 47 invoices with 3 deductions and a $12K overpayment.

DEMO OBJECTIVE: Show Atlas achieving 94%+ auto-match rate and resolving the complex GlobalTech payment in seconds, turning a week-long month-end crunch into a same-day close.

AGENTS INVOKED: OTC-AGT-009 (Cash Application & Reconciliation Agent), OTC-AGT-006 (Billing & Collections Agent)
