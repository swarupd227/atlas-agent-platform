. Customer Credit & Risk Assessment Agent
OTC-AGT-003  |  Category: Risk Management
4.1 Description
Continuously evaluates customer creditworthiness using internal payment history, external credit bureau data, and real-time financial signals. Dynamically adjusts credit limits, recommends payment terms, and flags high-risk accounts for review. Proactively identifies deteriorating credit conditions before they impact order flow.
4.2 Workflow Steps
22.	1. Trigger: new customer onboarding, periodic review, or order-triggered check
23.	2. Retrieve internal payment history (DSO, aging, past-due frequency)
24.	3. Pull external credit data (D&B, Experian, credit bureau scores)
25.	4. Analyze financial statements if available (public filings, bank references)
26.	5. Calculate composite risk score using weighted model
27.	6. Determine recommended credit limit and payment terms
28.	7. Compare against current exposure (open orders + open invoices)
29.	8. Flag accounts exceeding risk thresholds for human review
30.	9. Update credit record in ERP/CRM
31.	10. Notify sales and order management of credit decisions
32.	11. Schedule next periodic review based on risk tier

4.3 Common Ontology
The following entities and attributes form the shared data model referenced by this agent:
Entity	Key Attributes
Credit Score	internal score, external score, composite score, trend direction
Credit Limit	recommended, approved, utilized, available
Payment History	average days to pay, on-time %, dispute rate
Risk Tier	low, medium, high, watch list, credit hold
Financial Signal	revenue trend, profit margin, debt ratio, news sentiment
Exposure	open orders value, open AR value, total exposure

4.4 Claude Agent Skills
The following skills should be developed and made available as context for this agent:
Skill Name	Description
Credit Bureau Integration Skill	Fetches D&B, Experian, or Equifax commercial reports
Payment Pattern Analysis Skill	Analyzes historical payment behavior and trend detection
Financial Statement Parser Skill	Extracts key ratios from uploaded financial statements
Risk Scoring Model Skill	Composite scoring with configurable weights and thresholds
News & Signal Monitoring Skill	Scans financial news for distress signals (layoffs, lawsuits, downgrades)
Credit Decision Explanation Skill	Generates human-readable rationale for credit decisions

4.5 Eval Dataset
Recommended evaluation data and benchmarks for validating agent accuracy and performance:
•	Historical credit decisions with actual payment outcomes (default/non-default)
•	Customers across all risk tiers with known credit scores and limits
•	Edge cases: rapid growth companies, seasonal businesses, conglomerates
•	False positive/negative analysis: accounts flagged incorrectly in past
•	Stress test scenarios: economic downturn simulations on portfolio
•	Benchmark: decisions should match expert credit analyst 90%+ of the time

4.6 Production Runbooks
Operational procedures for handling common issues and failure scenarios:
Scenario	Procedure
Credit Bureau API Down	Fallback to cached scores with staleness warning; manual review queue
Mass Credit Limit Breach	Bulk processing procedure during economic events
False Credit Hold	Expedited release process with override authority levels
Customer Dispute of Credit Decision	Escalation path, supporting documentation requirements
Periodic Review Backlog	Prioritization by exposure size, triage procedure
Credit Model Drift	Monitoring procedure, recalibration triggers, model retraining
