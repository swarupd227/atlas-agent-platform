SCN-HNP-2 Subscriber Intelligence & Churn Prevention

Sub-segment: Metro Digital Subscriptions — Audience Retention | Newsrooms: Houston Chronicle, San Antonio Express-News

Business Problem
Hurricane Mara creates an acute subscriber management challenge that plays out across every major weather event, election night, and breaking news cycle at HNP. During a crisis, digital subscriptions spike dramatically as readers seek trusted local coverage. But the post-event drop-off is equally sharp: readers who subscribed during the storm cancel within 30–60 days once the acute news cycle ends, reasoning they ‘got what they needed.’ HNP currently has no systematic mechanism to identify these at-risk subscribers, personalise their post-event content experience, or trigger retention offers before they cancel. By the time the cancellation request arrives, the opportunity is gone.

The Houston Chronicle has 280,000 digital subscribers. 23% are in storm-affected zip codes. Historical data from Hurricane Harvey shows that cancellation rates in storm-affected zip codes spike 340% in weeks 3–6 post-event. Atlas must identify the at-risk cohort now — while they are still engaged — and execute a personalised retention sequence before the disengagement pattern takes hold.

4.  HNP-SUB-02 (Churn Prediction Engine) produces individual risk scores for each subscriber in the amber and red cohorts. Show two sample subscribers: one with 87% 60-day churn probability (subscribed 6 months ago, had already reduced to 1 session/week, storm-driven spike is temporary), one with 34% risk (18 months tenure, steady 4–5 sessions/week, lives in flood-affected area).
5.  HNP-SUB-03 (Personalised Re-engagement Generator) activates for the amber and red cohorts. Show three content sequences being generated in parallel: (a) storm-driven new subscribers receive a ‘This is what the Chronicle does for Houston’ sequence — surfacing 3 non-storm stories from their inferred interest areas to demonstrate year-round value; (b) red-cohort subscribers in flooded zip codes receive a 30-day free extension offer plus a curated ‘Recovery guide for your neighbourhood’ content package; (c) red-cohort price-sensitive subscribers receive a reduced-rate offer triggered only after two days of no engagement.
6.  Show the editorial approval gate: Audience Editor Review. The Audience Editor reviews the content sequence logic for each cohort (not every individual email — the cohort-level rules). She approves sequences for cohorts (a) and (c), modifies cohort (b) — ‘add the flood assistance resource links from the county’ — and the pipeline updates the template.

⚙ Platform Intelligence Setup in Atlas
Outcome Contract
Contract Name: HNP Subscriber Churn Prevention — Post-Event Retention
Risk Tier: Medium — personalised subscriber outreach; brand voice and offer accuracy matter
KPIs: At-Risk Cohort Identification Time (<4 hours post-event) | 60-Day Retained Rate for Amber Cohort (target: >65% vs. 45% baseline) | New-Subscriber 90-Day Retention (target: >55% vs. 38% baseline) | Revenue Retained per Pipeline Run
Drift Threshold: Cohort retained rate drops below baseline for two consecutive events triggers re-engagement sequence review

Knowledge Bases (KB System)
•  HNP Subscriber Behavioural History — 24-month engagement history for Houston Chronicle digital subscribers: session data, content consumption patterns, notification interactions, payment history, past churn/re-subscription events
•  HNP Brand Voice Guide — Editorial tone standards for subscriber-facing communications: what the Chronicle sounds like vs. a generic media brand. Prohibits artificial urgency language; requires genuine local connection.
•  HNP Retention Playbook — Historical retention campaign performance data: which offers worked for which cohorts in past events (Harvey 2017, Winter Storm Uri 2021, COVID surge 2020). Evidence base for cohort-specific intervention design.

MCP Server Integrations

•  HNP Churn Model MCP — get_churn_score, get_feature_importance, get_cohort_risk_distribution
•  HNP Geo MCP — classify_zip_by_storm_impact, get_flood_zone_data, get_neighbourhood_profile
•  HNP Content API MCP — get_articles_by_interest_profile, get_recovery_resource_content, get_section_top_stories

Governance Policies
Audience Editor Approval Gate — all re-engagement sequences require cohort-level editorial review before activation; no automated sends without approval
Offer Authority Boundary — agents can propose but not activate subscription price changes; all offer activations require confirmation from subscription operations
No Dark Pattern Policy — re-engagement copy reviewed against HNP brand voice guide; artificial scarcity, countdown timers, and misleading subject lines are blocked
