Employment Compliance & Policy Advisory Agent
LIT-AGT-001  |  Category: HR Compliance
2.1 Description
Provides real-time, multi-jurisdiction employment law guidance for handbook policies, HR procedures, and day-to-day compliance questions. This agent maps to Littler's Handbooks & Policies and HR Advice & Counsel practice areas. It monitors legislative changes across all 50 U.S. states and international jurisdictions, identifies policy gaps, drafts compliant policy language, and alerts clients to regulatory developments that require immediate action. Designed to function as the AI backbone of a Littler GPS-style compliance platform.
2.2 Workflow Steps
1.	1. Receive compliance inquiry (policy question, handbook review, new regulation alert)
2.	2. Identify applicable jurisdictions based on client footprint (states, countries, municipalities)
3.	3. Query employment law knowledge base for current statutes, regulations, and case law
4.	4. Cross-reference client's existing policies against current legal requirements
5.	5. Identify compliance gaps and risks, ranked by severity and potential liability
6.	6. Generate jurisdiction-specific policy language recommendations with citations
7.	7. Flag conflicting requirements across jurisdictions and recommend harmonization approach
8.	8. Route novel or high-risk questions to appropriate Littler attorney for review
9.	9. Generate compliance summary memo for client's legal/HR team
10.	10. Update monitoring watchlist for ongoing legislative and regulatory tracking
11.	11. Archive advisory in matter management system with time/billing codes

2.3 Common Ontology
The following entities and attributes form the shared data model referenced by this agent:
Entity	Key Attributes
Jurisdiction	state, municipality, country, effective date, enforcement agency
Policy	policy type, current version, applicable jurisdictions, last reviewed date, gaps
Statute	citation, title, effective date, amendment history, enforcement penalties
Regulation	agency, rule number, applicability criteria, compliance deadline
Client Profile	entity name, locations, headcount by jurisdiction, industry, union status
Advisory	question, analysis, recommendation, jurisdiction, attorney reviewer, confidence level

2.5 Eval Dataset
Recommended evaluation data and benchmarks for validating agent accuracy and performance:
•	1,000+ historical compliance inquiries with known correct answers and applicable statutes
•	Multi-state conflict scenarios: states with contradictory requirements on same topic (e.g., paid leave, cannabis, non-compete)
•	New legislation response time: correctly identifying impact within 24 hours of enactment
•	Policy drafting accuracy: generated language validated by Littler attorneys across 20+ topics
•	Jurisdiction identification edge cases: remote workers, multi-state employees, temporary assignments
•	Regression suite: previously incorrect advisories as negative examples

2.7 Regulatory Compliance & Policies
Applicable regulations and compliance requirements:
•	Attorney-Client Privilege: All advisories must maintain privilege markers and handling protocols
•	Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL): Agent outputs framed as research assistance, not legal advice without attorney review
•	State Bar Ethics Rules: Compliance with jurisdiction-specific rules on technology-assisted legal services
•	Confidentiality: Client data isolation, no cross-client data leakage, encryption at rest and in transit
•	Model Rules of Professional Conduct: Rules 1.1 (Competence), 1.6 (Confidentiality), 5.3 (Supervision of Nonlawyers)
•	Data Retention: Matter-specific retention schedules per client engagement letter and firm policy
