Dealer Experience Hub Conversational Agent
This is a Nous-original extension on top of Solifi Experience Hub — built to demonstrate the kind of customer-specific agent that Nous can deliver in 8–12 weeks on AgentCore.
Aligned Solifi modules	Experience Hub, Wholesale Finance, Originations, Portfolio Management
Primary persona	Dealers (external users); dealer support teams; Head of Wholesale
Business problem	Dealers are Solifi customers' most operationally intense channel. They want status updates, payoff quotes, payment confirmations, audit responses, and credit-application status — and they want them at all hours, on any device, without sitting in a phone queue. Most dealer portals deliver information but not assistance. The result is high dealer support volume, slow response times, and dealer-experience scores that drift downward.
Agent design — inputs, tools, decisions, outputs	Inputs: natural-language dealer questions, dealer identity and entitlements, current portfolio state across modules. Tools: Experience Hub APIs spanning Wholesale, Originations, Portfolio; Knowledge Assistant for policy and product Q&A; transactional tools for action requests (payoff requests, audit responses, payment confirmation). Decisions: which information to surface, which actions to enable, when to hand off to a human. Outputs: conversational responses, transactional outcomes, escalation handoffs.
Demo flow narrative	Sign in as a dealer (Pacific Powersports). Ask in natural language: 'What's the status of my floorplan? Are any units flagged?' The agent surfaces inventory state, two units 15 days past expected sale, and recommends an action. Ask: 'Can you generate a payoff quote for VIN 1HGCM82633A123456?' The agent calculates, presents the quote, and offers to email it. Ask: 'When does my next audit cycle hit?' The agent checks Use Case 4's data and answers. Demonstrate handoff: 'I want to discuss restructuring my line.' The agent collects context and routes to a human relationship manager.
Human-in-the-loop checkpoints	Information queries are agent-self-served. Transactional actions over a threshold require human approval. Anything that could be construed as advice routes to a human.
Quantified value hypothesis	60–75% reduction in dealer support inbound call volume. Dealer satisfaction score lift of 15–25 NPS points. Relationship managers freed up for value-creating conversations.
Nous accelerators used	Atlas conversational-agent framework; Anthropic CoE multi-tool dealer agent template; Experience Hub embedded chat surface reference build (extending our earlier Hearst and AURA demos).
