Demo 1: Intelligent Quote Configuration
PROBLEM STATEMENT: NovaTech's largest customer, Meridian Manufacturing ($28M annual spend), sends an RFQ for a complex equipment package: 3 product lines, 47 SKUs, tiered volume pricing, a custom bundle with compatibility constraints, multi-site delivery (4 plants), and a request for 12% discount off list - which exceeds the sales rep's 8% authority. Today this quote takes a senior sales engineer 4-6 hours to configure, price, and route for approval. Errors in manual pricing cause 3-5% revenue leakage.

DEMO OBJECTIVE: Show Atlas configuring the full quote in 90 seconds - product configuration with compatibility checks, intelligent pricing with discount optimization, approval routing, and professional quote document generation.

AGENTS INVOKED: OTC-AGT-001 (Quote & Configuration Agent), OTC-AGT-011 (Contract & Pricing Compliance Agent)
