Demo Step 5: Fatigue Protection (0:33–0:38)
Navigate to Screen 5. Focus on the HOLD impact panel.
TALK TRACK
This is the most counterintuitive result and the most important. Subscribers who were HELD — who received no email on a given day — had a 41% open rate on their next email, versus 29% for subscribers who were not held. That’s a 43% lift from NOT sending. The unsub rate dropped 50% since Atlas went live. And we can quantify the value: 12,400 subscribers who would have unsubscribed are still active, worth $186,000 in annual revenue. The best email is sometimes no email. No brand team will make that call for their own newsletter. Atlas makes it for the subscriber.
