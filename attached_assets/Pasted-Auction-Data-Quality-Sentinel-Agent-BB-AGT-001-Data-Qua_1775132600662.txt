Auction Data Quality Sentinel Agent
BB-AGT-001  |  Data Quality & Integrity  |  Scenario 1
2.1 Description
Continuously monitors every incoming auction transaction for statistical anomalies, data quality issues, and suspected fraud patterns. Processes 140,000+ daily transactions from Manheim, Adesa, independent auctions, and dealer-direct feeds, applying real-time statistical tests at the individual VIN level. Detects price outliers (>3 sigma from expected value), geographic pricing inconsistencies, suspicious multi-transaction VIN patterns (title-washing, odometer fraud), and volume anomalies that may indicate data feed issues or market disruption. This agent is the first line of defense for Black Book valuation accuracy, quarantining bad data before it can pollute the pricing model.
2.2 Workflow Steps
•	1. Ingest real-time auction transaction feed (VIN, auction house, sale date, sale price, region, seller/buyer type, condition)
•	2. Decode VIN; enrich with vehicle history; retrieve current Black Book valuation and expected price range
•	3. Apply statistical anomaly tests: Z-score against segment mean, IQR outlier detection, Mahalanobis distance for multivariate outliers
•	4. Check geographic pricing inconsistency: same VIN/segment priced >25% differently across regions without condition justification
•	5. Check suspicious VIN patterns: same VIN transacted 3+ times in 30 days (title-washing/odometer fraud signal)
•	6. Check volume anomalies: segment transaction volume deviating >2 sigma from rolling 4-week average
•	7. Classify flagged anomalies: Price Outlier, Geographic Inconsistency, Suspected Fraud, Volume Anomaly, Data Feed Issue
•	8. For suspected fraud: auto-quarantine from pricing model immediately (Full Auto autonomy)
•	9. For price/geographic outliers: flag for analyst review with supporting evidence and deviation magnitude
•	10. Log all decisions in Resolution Ledger with source citations (transaction IDs, comparables, statistical test results)
•	11. Feed clean transactions to valuation model pipeline; route quarantined to fraud investigation queue
•	12. Generate daily data quality report: transactions processed, anomalies flagged, quarantined, resolved

3. Market Shift Detector Agent
BB-AGT-002  |  Market Intelligence  |  Scenario 1
3.1 Description
Analyzes daily valuation movements across all vehicle segments (Compact Car, Mid-Size SUV, Full-Size Pickup, Luxury, EV) to detect accelerating depreciation or appreciation trends 2-4 weeks before they surface in standard weekly reports. Fuses structured data (auction prices, retail listings, days-on-lot, dealer inventory levels) with unstructured signals (OEM incentive announcements, recall news, tariff/trade policy changes, fuel price movements, economic indicators from FRED). This is Black Book competitive edge: detecting where the market is going before competitors see it.
3.2 Workflow Steps
•	1. Compute daily segment-level metrics: avg wholesale price, transaction volume, days-on-lot, retail listing price, bid-to-sale ratio
•	2. Calculate rolling 3-week, 6-week, and 12-week moving averages for each metric by segment
•	3. Apply change-point detection algorithms (CUSUM, PELT) to identify acceleration/deceleration in depreciation/appreciation trends
•	4. Ingest unstructured signals: OEM newsfeeds for incentive changes, NHTSA for recalls, trade policy sources, EIA for fuel prices, FRED for economic indicators
•	5. Classify each unstructured signal by: affected vehicle segments, directional impact, magnitude estimate, confidence level
•	6. Fuse structured trend data with unstructured signals using weighted evidence model to produce composite shift signals
•	7. Calculate lead time estimate: how many weeks ahead of standard weekly report would this shift become apparent
•	8. Generate market shift alert: affected segments, trend direction/magnitude, supporting evidence, confidence level, projected valuation impact
•	9. Route alerts: AMBER (emerging trend, 2+ weeks lead time) to Auto+Notify; RED (confirmed shift) to Confirm Before
•	10. Feed confirmed shift intelligence to Narrative Insight Generator (BB-AGT-004) for weekly report inclusion
•	11. Archive all alerts with subsequent validation data for continuous model improvement

4. Competitive Intelligence Monitor Agent
BB-AGT-003  |  Competitive Analysis  |  Scenario 1
4.1 Description
Tracks competitor vehicle valuation sources (KBB, NADA Guides, Manheim Market Report) for systematic divergence from Black Book values. When a segment shows growing divergence, the agent investigates whether Black Book is ahead of the market (good - detecting a shift earlier) or lagging (risk - competitor caught something BB missed). Produces weekly competitive positioning reports. Critical for protecting Black Book market position as the most trusted valuation source.
4.2 Workflow Steps
•	1. Ingest competitor valuation data from licensed sources: KBB trade-in values, NADA clean/rough trade, Manheim Market Report
•	2. Normalize competitor values to comparable basis: wholesale vs. retail, condition grade alignment, regional adjustment
•	3. Calculate divergence metrics by segment: absolute (dollar and percent), directional (same or opposite), trend (widening/narrowing)
•	4. Classify divergence patterns: (a) BB Leading - moved first, (b) BB Lagging - competitors moved first, (c) Systematic Disagreement
•	5. For Lagging patterns: trigger deep investigation - which signals are competitors seeing that BB is not?
•	6. For Leading patterns: document as competitive advantage evidence for sales and client communications
•	7. Generate weekly competitive positioning report: segment comparison, divergence trends, leadership/lagging classification
•	8. Flag any segment where BB diverges >5% from all competitors (potential methodology issue)
•	9. Monitor competitor product announcements and partnership news
•	10. Archive all competitive intelligence with date stamps for longitudinal analysis

5. Narrative Insight Generator Agent
BB-AGT-004  |  Content & Reporting  |  Scenario 1
5.1 Description
Synthesizes outputs from Agents 001-003 into Black Book weekly Wholesale Insights and Residual Values Insights reports published to customers. Generates data-backed market commentary per vehicle segment with visualizations, transforming 8-12 hours of analyst writing time per week into 3-minute draft generation with analyst review. Every claim backed by cited data sources. Operates under Confirm Before autonomy - analyst must review and approve before publication.
5.2 Workflow Steps
•	1. Aggregate weekly data: segment-level price movements, volume changes, days-on-lot trends, notable anomalies, market shift alerts
•	2. Rank segments by newsworthiness: largest price movements, confirmed market shifts, notable anomalies, competitive divergences
•	3. Generate Market Summary section: overall market direction, top movers, key drivers (economic, policy, seasonal)
•	4. Generate per-segment analysis: price trend narrative, volume analysis, notable transactions, forward outlook, risk flags
•	5. Generate EV Market Update section: Battery Adjusted Value trends, charging infrastructure developments, incentive impacts
•	6. Generate Outlook section: forward-looking signals from Market Shift Detector, policy pipeline, seasonal expectations
•	7. Auto-generate supporting visualizations: segment trend charts, heat maps, waterfall decompositions, YoY comparison tables
•	8. Insert source citations for every factual claim: auction transaction counts, economic data series, OEM references
•	9. Highlight sections requiring analyst judgment in yellow: subjective commentary, wide-confidence predictions
•	10. Format in Black Book standard template with header, section structure, and branding
•	11. Route to analyst for review with tracked changes capability
•	12. Upon approval, publish to customer channels; archive with analyst edits for future training
