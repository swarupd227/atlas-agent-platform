Demo 5: Dispute Resolution Intelligence
PROBLEM STATEMENT: Apex Industries, a $12M/year customer, has filed 12 disputes totaling $380K in the past 45 days - a sudden 400% increase from their historical dispute rate. The disputes cite pricing discrepancies between contracted rates and invoiced amounts. The AR team has been resolving disputes one-by-one without recognizing the pattern. A root cause analysis reveals the pricing discrepancy started exactly when a new contract took effect 45 days ago, suggesting a systemic contract loading error.

DEMO OBJECTIVE: Show Atlas detecting the dispute pattern, performing automated root cause analysis, identifying the systemic pricing error, calculating enterprise-wide exposure, and recommending bulk resolution.

AGENTS INVOKED: OTC-AGT-008 (Dispute Resolution Agent), OTC-AGT-006 (Billing & Collections Agent), OTC-AGT-011 (Contract & Pricing Compliance Agent)

Screen 6.1: Dispute Pattern Detection Dashboard
Atlas surfaces the anomalous dispute pattern that human analysts missed.
Layout: Anomaly Alert + Pattern Visualization + Root Cause Analysis
Top Banner - Pattern Alert: Red alert: "SYSTEMIC DISPUTE PATTERN DETECTED - Apex Industries: 12 disputes ($380K) in 45 days. Historical average: 1 dispute per quarter. Pattern: All disputes cite pricing discrepancy on products in Category C (Industrial Controls)." OTC-AGT-008 badge.
Center Left (50%) - Dispute Trend Visualization: A dual-axis chart. X-axis: time (12 months). Left Y-axis: dispute count (bar chart). Right Y-axis: dispute value (line chart). The last 45 days show a dramatic spike in both count and value for Apex Industries. A vertical dashed line marks "Contract MSA-2025-1104 Effective Date" - perfectly aligning with the spike onset. Below: a comparison panel showing Apex Industries' dispute rate vs. NovaTech portfolio average. The visual makes the correlation unmistakable.
Center Right (50%) - Automated Root Cause Analysis: OTC-AGT-008 and OTC-AGT-011 badges. Root cause investigation tree: Step 1: Classified 12 disputes - all Type: Pricing Discrepancy (100%). Step 2: Identified common factor - all involve Industrial Controls product category. Step 3: Cross-referenced contract MSA-2025-1104 effective Feb 12, 2026. Step 4: Compared contracted rates vs. invoiced rates - systematic 4.7% overcharge on all Category C products. Step 5: Root cause identified: "Contract pricing update for Category C was loaded with 2024 list prices instead of negotiated 2025 rates. ERP price list ID: PL-2024-C was applied instead of PL-2025-C-APEX." Confidence: 97%. Evidence expandable at each step.
Bottom Section - Exposure Assessment: OTC-AGT-011 badge. Enterprise impact analysis: Affected invoices (Apex only): 34 invoices, $814K total, $38.3K overcharged. Affected invoices (all customers on similar contracts): Scan in progress... Result: 3 additional customers affected - $127K total overcharge across 89 additional invoices. Total systemic exposure: $165K across 4 customers and 123 invoices. "View Full Exposure Report" CTA.

Screen 6.2: Bulk Resolution & Credit Processing
Atlas proposes and executes bulk resolution across all affected invoices.
Layout: Resolution Plan + Approval + Execution
Top Section - Resolution Recommendation: OTC-AGT-008 recommended resolution: (1) Issue credit memos for all 123 overcharged invoices totaling $165K. (2) Correct ERP price list: replace PL-2024-C with PL-2025-C-APEX (and variants for 3 other customers). (3) Rebill affected invoices at correct rates. (4) Send proactive apology and credit notification to all 4 affected customers. (5) Implement contract pricing validation rule to prevent recurrence. Estimated processing time: 2 hours (automated). Customer relationship impact: Positive (proactive resolution before further complaints).
Center Section - Affected Invoice Table: A master table of all 123 affected invoices: Customer, Invoice #, Invoice Date, Original Amount, Correct Amount, Credit Amount, Status. Grouped by customer with subtotals. Batch action: "Approve All Credits" with approval workflow (requires Finance VP for credits > $50K). Individual override capability.
Bottom Section - Prevention & Root Cause Fix: Two panels side-by-side: (Left) ERP Price List Correction: showing the specific data fix needed with before/after values and a "Submit Change Request" CTA that integrates with the ERP change management system. (Right) New Validation Rule: "Contract Pricing Verification Agent Rule: When a new contract is loaded, automatically compare invoiced prices against contracted rates for the first 10 invoices. Alert if variance exceeds 1%." Toggle to enable. This is the systemic fix that prevents recurrence.
