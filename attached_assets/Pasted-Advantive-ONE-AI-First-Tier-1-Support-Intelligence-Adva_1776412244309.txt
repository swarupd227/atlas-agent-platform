Business Problem
Advantive supports customers across 8+ product lines (Kiwiplan, DDI System, ParityFactory, InfinityQS, Pepperi, VeraCore, Advantzware, Proplanner) with a human-staffed Tier 1 support function handling high volumes of routine inquiries. The Advantive ONE Support Agent provides the interface layer; but transforming T1 support from human-resourced to AI-driven requires multi-agent workflow infrastructure for intent classification, diagnostic reasoning, and escalation packaging — capabilities Atlas ALMP is built for.
Demo Narrative & Workflow
Atlas deploys the multi-agent workflow infrastructure behind Advantive ONE's AIVA Support Agent — a four-agent pipeline that classifies every inbound query, resolves 60–65% autonomously, applies diagnostic reasoning for technical troubleshooting, and packages the remaining cases as enriched escalations for Tier 2 specialists. The result: dramatically reduced human labor dependency with improved customer experience.
9.	Triage & Intent Classifier receives inbound query from AIVA Support Agent; classifies intent (how-to, bug report, configuration issue, billing, product-specific troubleshooting); identifies the product and version; routes to the appropriate resolution agent.
10.	Knowledge Resolution Agent searches the full Advantive product knowledge base — product docs, historical ticket resolutions, and Advantive ONE's native product knowledge layer — generates a precise product-specific answer; self-assesses confidence score.
11.	Diagnostic Reasoning Agent handles technical issues that require product log analysis (via Advantive ONE's Product Log Intelligence capability): ingests error context, queries product logs, applies diagnostic reasoning per product, generates a step-by-step resolution path.
12.	T1→T2 Escalation Packager handles cases that cannot be resolved autonomously: generates a structured escalation package with full conversation context, classification, diagnostic steps taken, confidence rationale, and recommended T2 specialist routing — with Salesforce ticket pre-created via Advantive ONE's Salesforce integration.

  Advantive ONE Salesforce MCP: Salesforce case creation and bidirectional sync from Advantive ONE (built in Project 2 Component 3) — used by SUP-004 Escalation Packager
Governance Policies
  Confidence Gate Policy — any resolution with confidence score <0.75 is escalated or flagged for human review; Atlas never delivers uncertain answers as confident
  Customer Privacy Policy — customer ticket data used only for resolution; never used to train third-party models without Advantive consent
  KB Update Policy — any product release triggers automated KB update within 24 hours of release; KB quality evaluated before update is activated
